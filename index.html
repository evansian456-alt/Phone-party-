📖 About Phone Party

What is Phone Party?

Phone Party is a music synchronization app that allows multiple phones to play music together in perfect sync. It's designed for parties and social gatherings where you want to create a massive sound experience by connecting everyone's phones.

🎵 How to Start and Join a Party

  1. Start a Party: Click "Create Party", choose your DJ name, optionally select music, and click "Start the party"
  2. Share the Code: Share your 6-character party code with friends
  3. Join a Party: Click "Join Party", enter the party code, and optionally add your nickname
  4. Everyone Syncs: Music plays in perfect sync across all connected devices

🎧 Source Options

  • Local Device: Play music files from your phone (MP3, WAV, M4A, etc.)
  • Another App: Stream from Spotify, YouTube, or other apps (audio will sync to all devices)
  • Microphone Mode: Broadcast live audio from your device microphone

💬 Chat Modes

  • OPEN: Guests can send text messages and emojis (Party Pass/Pro only)
  • EMOJI ONLY: Guests can only send emoji reactions
  • LOCKED: All guest messaging is disabled

🎯 Plans & Features

  • Free Plan: Up to 2 phones, unlimited time, audio sync only - no messaging or reactions
  • Party Pass (£3.99): One-time unlock for one party - 2-hour session, up to 4 phones, chat + emoji reactions, DJ quick message buttons, auto party prompts
  • Pro Monthly (£9.99): Unlimited time, up to 10 phones, custom messages, all reactions, no ads, priority support, advanced analytics

Party Pass unlocks Pro features for all guests in that party for 2 hours.

🏆 How Scoring Works

  • DJ Score: Earn points based on party duration, guest engagement, and reactions
  • Guest Points: Earn contribution points by sending reactions and messages
  • Live Leaderboard: See top contributors in real-time during the party

🔒 Safety & Privacy

  • No Music Included: Users provide their own music files
  • Local Network: Works best on home Wi-Fi or hotspots
  • Host Controls: Party host controls playback and can moderate chat
  • Anonymous by Default: Guests appear as "Guest 1", "Guest 2" etc.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Users are responsible for their music source and content
  • Best experience with same Wi-Fi network or mobile hotspot
  • Host can control chat permissions
  • Party expires after 2 hours (or when host ends it)
